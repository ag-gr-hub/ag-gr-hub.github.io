WWE News About Royal Rumble

Charlotte Flair can help build the WWE women's midcard



Raphael Garcia Janurary 31,2025 8:00 AM EST

The WWE Royal Rumble is coming in hot and there’s hope for a lot of surprises. Fans want to see the women’s rumble, because there’s opportunity for several big names to return to the show. Speculation is that Charlotte Flair is one of those performers. That speculation also predicts that she’s going right back to the top of the women’s division with c hampionship gold around her waist. If Flair does return, one must wonder where she stands and is best used on the main roster today.

Charlotte Flair is a star. In all aspects of professional wrestling, Flair is a star. It does not matter what promotion she’d be in; she would still be booked as a top name in the women’s division. WWE has long positioned her at the top of the women’s division, even if that meant lowering other fan favorites along the way. So, no one should be surprised if someone’s momentum is cut because of Flair’s return.

Fightful Select recently reported that a WrestleMania match in sight pits Flair against new WWE Women’s Champion, Tiffany Stratton. While Stratton versus Flair has the allure of a main event contest, WWE must be careful with the outcome. Think back to when Asuka moved to the main roster after dominating WWE NXT. She’d win the Royal Rumble and face Flair at WrestleMania, only to lose. Some will argue that she never fully regained her momentum after that moment. While WWE’s booking at the time is to blame, the company does not want to make that same mistake with another young talent in Tiffany Stratton.


Roman Reigns makes the 2025 WWE Royal Rumble

Roman Reigns is in his first Royal Rumble in five years and he makes this match an even bigger moment.

By Raphael Garcia | Jan 28, 2025

The WWE Royal Rumble is only a few days away. Fans are speculating about what will happen, as it seems like the events are harder to predict than normal. The men's rumble feels especially big this year, as several of the top names are announced for the match. One such individual is Roman Reigns. His inclusion in the battle royal makes everything even more important this year.

On Saturday, February 1, Roman Reigns will enter the Royal Rumble for the first time in five years. The last time he stepped into the match, he was the final person eliminated. Drew McIntyre tossed him over the ropes in a step to set up his run to the WWE Championship. But it was Reigns who has been the dominating force since, and the most important person on the WWE roster.

Now, he doesn't have the WWE Undisputed Championship and he's on a quest to win it back. Should he be the favorite for the match? Depends on how you look at the rest of the field and the stories WWE is telling. But Reigns is certainly going to have several big moments on Saturday.

Every interaction that Reigns has in this match has the potential to lead to something big. Will he and CM Punk have a face-off moment? Who does Reigns eliminate and will they turn their focus to him? Who eliminates Reigns and gets the big momentum boost from that moment? Does he have a moment with a surprise entrant like Joe Hendry or Josh Alexander? WWE Creative have done such a great job building this Royal Rumble as a moment in which so many different things can happen, there is no way to predict how things will go down.



Jordynne Grace signs with WWE

WWE landed a major name in professional wrestling, signing former TNA Knockouts Champion to a multi-year contract.



By Raphael Garcia | Jan 28, 2025

The Free Agency race of 2025 has started. On January 27 Fightful Select reported WWE snatched the first major name off the board. Former TNA Knockouts Champion, Jordynne Grace is officially signed to the biggest wrestling promotion in the industry.

According to Sean Ross Sapp, Grace has signed a multi-year contract. This was one of the worst-kept secrets in the business, as Grace had made multiple appearances in WWE during the partnership with TNA Wrestling. According to the report “WWE made the offer a couple of weeks before Grace finished up.”

Grace is a huge signing for the WWE women’s division and she comes at the perfect time. The 2025 WWE Royal Rumble is a few short days away, and the women’s division needs a big name to light a fire in the group. There are reports that Becky Lynch and Charlotte Flair are both poised for a return, and it would be interesting to see Grace make her return to the company the same night. There’s also a chance she will return to WWE NXT, as there are several names there who she could have great matches and feuds with. But many believe that Grace is main roster ready, and her successful 14-year career proves that point.
